Hi, I'm Julie!

I’m a rising junior (c/o ‘23) double majoring in Computer Science and Studio Art at Wellesley College. I’m an aspiring Product Designer looking to create positive, intuitive, and beautiful experiences for people! I’m particularly interested in designing for B2C companies, as I love working on products that people can use and interact with daily and/or casually.

I found my dream occupation after I took a Human-Computer Interaction class the summer of my sophomore year. I entered that class as a computer science major and walked away as a designer with big ideas, visions, and aspirations.
