Activ

Putting the Activ in Activism

Activ is a “one-stop-shop” app that allows users to contribute to a cause or movement through signing petitions, donating, calling local and federal legislators, and more—all through a click of a button.

Introduction

Attending my first designathon

Last summer, I participated in my first designathon hosted by Hack Duke. Through this event, I was able to experience working on a short term project with students I've never met before and collaborate virtually. We placed runner up in the Inequality Track in the Advanced category.

The Problem

How can we propel rising social activism movements on social media platforms to maintain long term engagement and support?

2020 has seen the rise of social activism, especially on social media. Our app Activ hopes to maintain long-term engagement and support for various movements by compiling a list of activities and education material for people to effectively support the causes they care about.

Our Solution

A one-stop-shop for activism

Creating a user-oriented platform that aims at maintaining social awareness and social activism through presenting accessible, actively automated solutions such as petitions, protests and campaigns to be supported.
